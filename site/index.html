ABOUT HER

Ella Marija Lani Yelich-O'Connor, well known by her artistic name Lorde, is a composer and a singer. Elected the most influential young of the world by the Times magazine, nominated the “Woman of the Year” by MTV, on 2013. Became famous after her first hit “Royals”.
